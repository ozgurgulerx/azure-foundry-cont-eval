## Section 1: Company Overview

Contoso Solar is a renewable energy company founded in 2019 and headquartered in Austin, Texas. The company manufactures and installs residential and commercial solar panel systems across the United States. Contoso Solar has installed over 50,000 solar systems since its founding.

## Section 3: Installation Services

Contoso Solar offers professional installation for all products. Installation pricing:
- **Residential systems (up to 20 panels):** $3,000 flat fee
- **Residential systems (21-40 panels):** $5,500 flat fee
- **Commercial systems:** Custom quote required, starting at $10,000
- **PowerVault battery installation:** $1,500

Installation includes:
- Site assessment and engineering review
- Permitting and utility coordination
- Professional installation by certified technicians
- System commissioning and testing
- Connection to monitoring app

Average installation time:
- Residential: 1-2 days
- Commercial: 3-7 days

## Section 5: Financing

Contoso Solar offers three financing options:
1. **Cash purchase:** Pay in full, receive 5% discount
2. **Solar loan:** 12-year term, rates from 4.99% APR, no down payment required
3. **Solar lease:** 20-year lease, fixed monthly payment, Contoso Solar owns the system

Federal solar tax credit (Investment Tax Credit, ITC) may apply. Customers should consult a tax professional for eligibility. Contoso Solar does not provide tax advice.
